Paris, France


Paris is a world-famous city known for its romantic atmosphere, rich history, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture. It attracts millions of visitors every year with its art, architecture, and cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  4. Champs-Élysées – a world-renowned avenue for shopping
  5. Montmartre – known for its artistic history and views
  • Croissants – a classic French breakfast pastry
  • Crêpes – sweet and savory street food favorites
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate desserts
  • French Cheese – a wide variety of rich flavors
  • Ratatouille – a traditional vegetable dish
